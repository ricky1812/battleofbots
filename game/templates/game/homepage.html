RULES AND HOW TO PLAY!

Round 1

  • The event will have 30 questions.
  • Solving a question is necessary in order to proceed to the next one.
  • +10 will be awarded for each correct answer.
  • No negative marking for wrong answers.
  • Answers to be in lowercase with no spaces and special characters. Numbers should be in digits
  • Budget for the next round will depend on the number of questions answered.
  • Top 16 will get qualified for the main round.
